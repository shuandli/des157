Project topic

  1. My topic for the project is global warming and how to address it through sustainable living i.e. looking for sustainable alternatives to things that otherwise contribute to global warming. I am thinking of putting together an interactive virtual tour style interface where people can go through rooms of a hosue (or apartment) and select items. Selected items will display messages that propose sustainable alternatives. For example, clicking on a lamp could show a fluorescent lightbulb instead of an incondescent one.
