Comparative research

Power Shift Network

An offshoot of the Energy Action Coalition, Power Shift Network is more than anything an activist group focused on achieving economic equality through a greener environment. In other words, they believe they can provide jobs by increasing the amount of green activity in the US. Power Shift's actions within the last year include but are not limited to protesting at Standing Rock and attempting to launch "it.eco" as a platform for environmental groups to congregate.

Unesco: Green Citizens

The Unesco: Green Citizens is an exhibition intended to raise awareness on sustainable living and development for the sake of a cleaner planet in the future. They define sustainability as "meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs," and their exhibitions are located across the world. Users are able to submit their own stories about education in sustainability and they include projects ranging from sustainable rice farming to elementary school education in a green planet.
