As the house heats up in the summer, our first instinct is to crank up the air conditioning. While AC goes a long way into feeling comfortable, but it comes at a price. Two thirds of all American households have air conditioners which uses tons of power and emits literally hundreds of metric tons of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. You may hear carbon dioxide a lot when talking about climate change and global warming. Fans like the ones you clicked take up far less electricty to run at only a few tens of watts and will be friendlier to the planet and your family's electric bill.
