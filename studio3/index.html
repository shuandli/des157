What I [usually] keep on my person....

The panda hat I currently own is actually the third one I made. I do not recall where my first hat is, probably in my room somewhere at home home. It was not flat-brimmed, and ball caps tend to shrink my head. My brother has my second panda hat. The hat is a blank white cap with sharpie. I have not idea where the idea to draw on a hat came from, just though it would be a fun idea.

I love my glasses, but they keep sliding towards the bottom of my nose. I need to keep looking forward or keep my eyes wide open for them to stay in place. It is honestly not a big deal, provided it doesn't rain. When it rains, it's game over.

As if my portal wasn't an indication, I love Star Wars. Naturally I own a lightsaber. A collector's edition metal lightsaber that lights up and makes noises. It's silly, I know, but there's an undeniably great feeling just swinging a lightsaber around in a dark room. As a bonus, I have a new pick up line, "Hey girl. Wanna see my lightsaber collection?"
